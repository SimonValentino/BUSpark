Mission

The SparkED! project aims to solve the problem of low-income students who are struggling with school but can't afford a tutor along with providing an initiative for service hours for high schoolers. SparkED! connects high school and college students who want to tutor students through an online portal.

Personalized Learning

Tailored educational experiences for every student.

Gain Service Hours

Students will gain service hours for volunteering as tutors.

Connect and Collaborate

Connect with peers and educators for meaningful collaboration.
